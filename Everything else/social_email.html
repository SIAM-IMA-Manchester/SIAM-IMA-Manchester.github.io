Manchester SIAM Social Event

Hello everyone,

We are delighted to announce our first event of the year!

As the organising committee, we promise to provide you opportunities for interdisciplinary work across the university and industrial links! But would you like to know who we are? Join us for a relaxed evening to meet the Chapter members, have a say on future activities and have some free drinks and pizza!

The social event -- first of its kind! -- will be held in Frank Adams 1, Alan Turing Building at 6pm, on the 6th of February.

We have limited spaces, so make sure you register for the event here! Just to reiterate, you do need to be a member of the Chapter to attend!

Hope to see you all there!

-- Sophia, President of Manchester SIAM.
